# Support Resolver Agent — Marketplace Listing
## Updated: March 8, 2026

## Title
**Support Resolver Agent — Ticket Triage + Ready-to-Send Responses**

## Short Description
Classifies support tickets, drafts human-quality responses, and flags escalations — with built-in QA and policy compliance.

## Full Description

### What You Get (Per Ticket)
- **Classification**: category, severity, customer sentiment
- **Draft response**: ready to send, policy-compliant, under 150 words
- **Next actions**: concrete steps (clarify, provide steps, escalate, close)
- **Escalation flags**: automatic triggers for refund threats, legal, security, critical outages
- **Citations**: references to specific KB articles or policy sections
- **Confidence score**: honest assessment of response reliability

### What Makes This Different
This agent doesn't just summarize the ticket — it **resolves** it. Every response passes QA that checks:
- Reply addresses the actual issue
- No invented product features or policies
- Escalation rules are correctly applied
- Tone is professional regardless of customer mood

### Proven Results
- **100% pass rate** across 10 diverse tickets (billing, bug, shipping, refund threat, onboarding, password, feature request, complaint, upgrade, cancellation)
- **9.6s average per ticket** — classification + draft response + QA
- Correct escalation on all 3 high-severity cases (chargeback threat, duplicate billing, urgent account access)
- Handles 11 ticket categories automatically

### What I Need From You
1. Ticket text
2. Knowledge base content (optional — improves accuracy)
3. Policy docs (refund policy, SLA, etc. — optional)
