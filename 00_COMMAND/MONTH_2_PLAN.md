## Agent #5 Candidates (Pick One)

### Option A: Meeting Scheduler Agent
- **Job**: Given (participants, constraints, timezone) → produce (3 time slot options + calendar invite draft + follow-up sequence)
- **Market**: Sales teams, recruiters, consultants
- **Pricing**: $2.00/scheduling attempt
- **Why**: Natural cross-sell with Sales Ops clients

### Option B: Data Analyst Agent
- **Job**: Given (CSV/data + question) → produce (analysis summary + key metrics + chart descriptions + recommendations)
- **Market**: Small businesses, marketers, operators
- **Pricing**: $5.00/analysis
- **Why**: Higher price point, limited competition in AI labor space

### Option C: Social Media Manager Agent
- **Job**: Given (brand context + topic) → produce (7-day content calendar + 14 posts + hashtag strategy)
- **Market**: Small businesses, solopreneurs, marketing agencies
- **Pricing**: $4.00/calendar
- **Why**: Natural extension of Content Repurposer, recurring need

**Recommendation**: Option A (Meeting Scheduler) — highest synergy with Sales Ops, creates a bundle play: "We find your leads AND book the meetings."
