# FREELANCER.COM DOMINATION ROADMAP
> 20 Dedicated AI Agents — Ranked by Success Probability × Revenue × Low Risk

## Strategy

Freelancer.com rewards **speed, volume, and ratings**. Our edge: every gig is backed by a real multi-agent pipeline that delivers in seconds, not hours. The strategy is:

1. **Deploy all 20 gigs simultaneously** — cast a wide net
2. **Price aggressively on first 10 jobs per gig** — buy 5-star reviews
3. **Automate bid responses** — use proposal_writer agent to generate custom bids
4. **Track win rate per gig type** — kill losers at Day 14, double down on winners
5. **Graduate top earners to premium pricing** by Week 4

## RECOMMENDATIONS

### Pricing Strategy
- **Week 1-2**: Price at 30-50% below market to accumulate reviews fast
- **Week 3-4**: Raise to market rate once you have 5+ reviews per gig
- **Month 2+**: Premium pricing on gigs with >4.8 stars

### Bid Strategy
- **First line**: Reference something specific in THEIR project (not generic)
- **Second line**: State your capability in 1 sentence
- **Third line**: Deliverable + timeline
- **Always include**: "I can start immediately" (Freelancer buyers love urgency)
- Use the proposal_writer agent to generate custom bids — meta-automation

### Risk Mitigation
- **Bookkeeping (#12)**: Add disclaimer "for organizational purposes only, not professional accounting advice"
- **Business Plans (#18)**: "For planning purposes — financial projections are estimates"
- **Market Research (#17)**: "Based on publicly available data as of [date]"
- **Resume Writing (#15)**: Offer revision rounds to prevent disputes

### Gig Bundling (Cross-Sell)
- Lead Gen + Cold Email + CRM = "Full sales pipeline package"
- SEO Articles + Social Media + Email = "Content marketing bundle"
- Data Entry + Web Scraping + CRM = "Data operations bundle"
- Product Descriptions + Ad Copy + Social = "E-commerce launch bundle"

### Portfolio Building (Week 1 Priority)
- Run each agent once with demo data
- Save outputs as portfolio samples in output/freelancer_portfolio/
- Use real-looking but fictional company names
- Screenshot the JSON-to-deliverable conversion as proof of automation
