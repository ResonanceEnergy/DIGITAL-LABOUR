# FIVERR GIG #3 — CONTENT REPURPOSER (COPY/PASTE READY)

## Gig Title (80 chars max)
I will repurpose your blog post into LinkedIn Twitter email and Instagram using AI

## Category
Writing & Translation > Content Writing > Articles & Blog Posts

## Search Tags
content repurposing, blog to social media, linkedin post, twitter thread, content marketing, social media content, repurpose content, email newsletter

## Gig Description

**You write the content. I turn it into 5 platform-ready formats.**

Stop spending 2+ hours reformatting every blog post for social media. I run an AI agent pipeline that analyzes your content and produces platform-specific versions — each one QA-verified before delivery.

**What you get per piece:**
- LinkedIn post (professional tone, hook + insight format, 150-200 words)
- Twitter/X thread (4-6 tweets, punchy, under 280 chars each)
- Email newsletter version (subject line + formatted body)
- Instagram caption (casual tone + relevant hashtags)
- Executive summary (3 sentences, boardroom-ready)

**What makes this different:**
Every output is adapted for platform conventions — not just shortened or copy-pasted. A dedicated QA agent checks tone, length, and structural compliance before I deliver.

**I need from you:**
1. Blog post URL or text
2. Target audience (optional)
3. Brand voice notes (optional — e.g. "casual", "authoritative", "witty")

**Turnaround:** Single pieces in minutes. Bulk orders same day.

## Pricing Packages

### Basic — $10
- 1 content piece → 5 platform formats
- QA-verified output
- 1 revision
- Delivery: Same day

### Standard — $75
- 10 content pieces → 5 formats each
- CSV/JSON export option
- 2 revisions per piece
- Delivery: 1-2 days

### Premium — $200
- 20 content pieces/month
- All 5 formats per piece
- Priority delivery
- Unlimited revisions
- Delivery: Ongoing monthly

## FAQ

**Q: What formats work as input?**
A: Blog posts, articles, case studies, whitepapers, newsletters, press releases, podcast transcripts — basically any text content.

**Q: Can you match my brand voice?**
A: Yes. Provide brand voice notes or 2-3 examples of content you like, and the agent adapts tone, vocabulary, and style.

**Q: What about images for Instagram?**
A: I deliver the caption and hashtags. You pair it with your own visuals. The caption is written to complement, not replace, the image.

**Q: Do you post it for me?**
A: No — I deliver the content in your preferred format (text, CSV, or JSON). You or your social media manager handles scheduling and posting.

# FIVERR GIG #4 — DOCUMENT EXTRACTOR (COPY/PASTE READY)

## Gig Title (80 chars max)
I will extract structured data from your documents invoices contracts using AI

## Category
Data > Data Processing > Data Entry

## Search Tags
data extraction, document processing, invoice extraction, PDF to data, OCR, contract parsing, data entry, document AI

## Gig Description

**Stop manually copying data from documents into spreadsheets.**

I run an AI agent that reads your documents, identifies key fields, and extracts them into clean structured data (JSON or CSV). Every extraction is QA-verified with a confidence score.

**What you get per document:**
- Auto-detected document type (invoice, contract, resume, report, etc.)
- All key fields extracted into structured JSON
- Confidence score for accuracy verification
- Extraction metadata (processing time, field count)

**Supported document types:**
- Invoices (vendor, line items, totals, dates, PO numbers)
- Contracts (parties, terms, dates, clauses, obligations)
- Resumes/CVs (contact info, experience, skills, education)
- Financial reports (figures, periods, comparisons)
- Medical/lab reports (patient info, results, ranges)
- Any structured or semi-structured document

**What makes this different:**
Not a generic OCR dump. The agent understands document structure and extracts meaningful fields. A QA agent validates accuracy and flags low-confidence extractions.

**I need from you:**
1. The document (PDF, image, or text)
2. What fields you need (optional — agent auto-detects if not specified)
3. Output format preference (JSON or CSV)

**Turnaround:** Single documents in seconds. Bulk orders same day.

## FAQ

**Q: What file formats do you accept?**
A: PDF, PNG, JPG, TIFF for scanned documents. TXT, DOCX, HTML for digital documents. If you have another format, message me and I'll confirm.

**Q: How accurate is the extraction?**
A: Each extraction comes with a confidence score (0-100%). Typical accuracy is 85-95% depending on document quality. Low-confidence fields are flagged for your review.

**Q: Can you handle handwritten documents?**
A: The agent works best with typed/printed documents. Handwritten documents may have lower accuracy — I'll let you know the confidence score and you can decide whether to proceed.

**Q: What about sensitive documents?**
A: I process documents through secure AI pipelines (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google). Documents are not stored after processing. If you have specific compliance requirements, message me to discuss.

**Q: Can this handle bulk monthly processing?**
A: Absolutely. Custom pricing for 100+ documents/month. Message me with your volume for a quote.
